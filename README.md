## About Me

My name is **Simon Lorenc** and I am a passionate **Fullstack Developer** with expertise in building scalable and maintainable applications. On the frontend, I specialize in **Angular**, leveraging its latest features to craft dynamic and user-friendly interfaces. For backend development, I use **Java** with modern frameworks like **Spring Boot**.

### Key Interests
- **Frontend**: Angular (latest versions), TypeScript, RxJS, NgRx.
- **Backend**: Java, Spring Framework, RESTful APIs, PostgreSQL/Oracle.
- **DevOps**: **Docker**, CI/CD automation, containerized applications.
- **Testing**: JUnit, SpectatorJS, Jest, ng-mocks, Cucumber, Playwright, Cypress.

### Passion for Automation
I thrive on simplifying development workflows through **CI/CD automation**, ensuring seamless deployments and maintaining high software quality. My focus is on creating robust pipelines that integrate **Docker** and modern development tools to streamline delivery.

I'm always eager to learn, experiment, and share knowledge about technologies that excite me, particularly **Angular** and **Docker**.

Feel free to explore my repositories and reach out for collaborations!
